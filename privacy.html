Privacy Policy

JLPT Quiz App is committed to respecting and protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains our practices regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of non-private data when you use our iOS app.

Information We Collect

JLPT Quiz App does not collect any personally identifiable information (PII) or private data from users. We do not track or store your name, email address, location, or any other personal information.

Non-Private Data

The only data we collect are non-private and anonymous usage data. This data helps us improve the app's performance and user experience. The non-private data we collect includes:

  • Device type and model
  • iOS version
  • App version
  • App usage statistics
  • Crash reports (anonymous)

Third-Party Services

JLPT Quiz App does not use any third-party services or analytics tools that collect private data or personally identifiable information.

Children's Privacy

JLPT Quiz App does not knowingly collect any data from children under the age of 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately, and we will delete such information from our records.
